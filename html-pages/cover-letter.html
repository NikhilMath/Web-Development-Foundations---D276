Dear Hiring Manager,

I am writing to express my interest in the IT Help Desk position in Washington, DC. With several years of hands-on experience supporting users, managing devices, and improving IT operations, I bring a practical and service-oriented approach to technology support.

In my current role as IT Support Manager at Visionary Homes, I support employees across macOS, Windows, and iOS environments. I manage device lifecycle operations, maintain Jamf and Apple Business Manager, and assist with onboarding new staff by provisioning and configuring workstations. I also provide direct help desk support, troubleshoot technical issues, and create internal documentation to make IT processes easier for employees to understand and use. A major focus of my work has been reducing friction for users by creating reliable, simple systems that allow employees to focus on their work rather than technology problems.

Previously, as IT Manager at Kimberbell, I worked closely with developers and IT specialists while helping maintain systems and support internal users. Earlier in my career as a Web Developer and IT Specialist, I developed a strong technical foundation that continues to support my troubleshooting and problem-solving abilities today.

Beyond my professional roles, I serve on the Board of Directors for Recovery Café, where I contribute through grant writing, volunteer coordination, social media management, and website development. This experience has strengthened my communication and organizational skills, which are essential when supporting users with varying levels of technical knowledge.

I am particularly interested in this opportunity in Washington, DC because I enjoy environments where technology support plays a critical role in helping organizations operate smoothly. I take pride in providing clear, patient assistance and ensuring that users feel supported and confident when working with technology.

Thank you for your time and consideration. I would welcome the opportunity to discuss how my experience and problem-solving approach could contribute to your IT team.
